# Support

Here is how to get help with the POS Service.

## Issues & Requests

- Submit bugs, feature requests, and questions via the repository issue tracker.
- Include tenant ID, outlet/device identifiers, reproduction steps, logs (sanitised), and timeframe.
- Label severity (`bug`, `enhancement`, `ops`, `security`).

## Internal Channels

- Slack:
  - `#bengobox-pos` – engineering discussions.
  - `#pos-ops` – operational coordination.
- Weekly triage: Fridays 11:00 EAT.
- Incident response handled through Opsgenie on-call rotations.

## External Customers & Partners

- Email `support@bengobox.com` with SLA tier, tenant, outlet, and incident summary.
- For urgent outages, escalate via customer success manager or hotline.

## Documentation

- `plan.md` – roadmap and sprint planning.
- `docs/erd.md` – data model reference.
- Future docs (integration guides, ADRs) will live under `docs/`.

## Service Levels

- Target uptime: 99.9%.
- P1 (cashier operations blocked): respond within 15 minutes, updates every 30 minutes.
- P2 (degraded but functioning): respond within 1 hour, updates every 2 hours.
- P3 (general support): respond within 1 business day.

We appreciate detailed tickets—they speed up diagnosis and resolution.
