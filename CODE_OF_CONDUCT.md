# Code of Conduct

The POS Service community is dedicated to a harassment-free, inclusive experience for everyone.

## Expectations

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Provide constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what benefits users, operators, and fellow engineers.
- Handle sensitive business information responsibly.

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, intimidation, discriminatory remarks, trolling, or disclosure of confidential information without approval.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers will enforce this Code and may take corrective action (warnings, temporary bans, permanent removal) for violations.

## Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behaviour, please email `conduct@bengobox.com`. Reports will be handled confidentially and promptly.

This policy is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org) v2.1.
